About

Welcome to my corner of the web!
I'm Bethuel Sarmoi, a passionate web developer with a knack for crafting elegant and functional digital experiences. By day, I'm dedicated to shaping young minds as a high school teacher, fostering a love for learning and empowering the next generation. By night (and weekends, and any spare moment really), I immerse myself in the dynamic world of web development.
Driven by a desire to merge creativity with technology, I specialize in front-end development, where I bring designs to life with clean code and intuitive user interfaces. Whether it's building responsive websites, developing interactive web applications, or optimizing user experiences, I thrive on the challenge of turning ideas into reality.
My journey into web development began as a personal passion, but it quickly evolved into a professional pursuit. With a foundation in education, I bring a unique perspective to my work, emphasizing clear communication, user-centric design, and continuous learning. I believe that great websites not only look good but also function seamlessly, providing value and delight to users across the digital landscape.
In addition to my technical skills, I'm a firm believer in collaboration and lifelong learning. I actively seek out opportunities to expand my knowledge, whether through online courses, workshops, or engaging with the vibrant developer community. I believe that the best solutions emerge through teamwork, creativity, and a willingness to embrace new technologies.
When I'm not coding or grading papers, you can find me exploring the great outdoors, experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, or losing myself in a good book. Life is an adventure, and I approach each project with the same sense of curiosity and enthusiasm.
Whether you're a fellow developer, a potential employer, or simply curious about the world of web development, I invite you to explore my portfolio and connect with me. Let's collaborate, innovate, and create something amazing together.
Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to connecting with you soon!

Project Details

Educational Resource Management System

Develop a web application or platform that assists teachers in managing educational resources effectively. This system could include features such as lesson planning tools, curriculum mapping, resource sharing among teachers, assessment creation and tracking, and student progress monitoring. By integrating user-friendly interfaces and customizable features, this project aims to streamline administrative tasks for teachers, allowing them to focus more on student engagement and learning outcomes.

Interactive Learning Platform

Create an interactive online learning platform that offers engaging educational content across various subjects and grade levels. Incorporate multimedia elements such as videos, interactive quizzes, and gamified activities to make learning more enjoyable and effective. Additionally, implement features like progress tracking, personalized recommendations, and discussion forums to facilitate student collaboration and self-paced learning. This project aims to provide a dynamic and accessible learning environment for students, complementing traditional classroom instruction and catering to diverse learning styles.

Parent-Teacher Communication App

Design and develop a mobile app or web-based platform that enhances communication between teachers and parents. This app could include features such as real-time messaging, event notifications, attendance tracking, academic performance updates, and appointment scheduling. By fostering transparent and timely communication, this project aims to strengthen the partnership between educators and parents, leading to better support for students' academic and social-emotional development. Additionally, incorporating user feedback mechanisms and analytics tools can help continually improve the app's functionality and user experience.
